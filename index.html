VoltLab — Account and Data Deletion

This page explains how to request account and data deletion in the VoltLab app.

Who we are

Developer: Denis Gladkii (Publisher of VoltLab)

App: VoltLab (air.ru.sever.ElectricalEngineering)

Support contact: de.gladkiy@gmail.com

How to delete your account

By email request: Please send an email from the address linked to your account to de.gladkiy@gmail.com with the subject line VoltLab Account Deletion. Include your user ID (if available in settings) and your country/region. We will confirm the request and begin the deletion process.

If you signed in using Google or Apple, deleting your VoltLab account will not delete your external Google/Apple account.

What data will be deleted

  • VoltLab account data (including internal user ID, email, display name if provided).
  • Synchronized user data/settings stored on the server (Supabase), if applicable.
  • Login and push notification tokens (OneSignal).
  • Analytics events linked to your identifier in statistics (AppMetrica) — anonymized or deleted according to the provider’s policy.

Local data on your device (cache, saved files) will be cleared during logout/deletion. Some log records may be kept temporarily for security and fraud prevention.

Deletion timeline

We process requests within 7 days. Full deletion and/or anonymization of data by third-party providers (e.g. OneSignal, AppMetrica) may take up to 30 days from confirmation.

Your rights

You may withdraw consent for analytics or personalized notifications at any time in the app settings. After account deletion, data recovery is not possible.

Types of data

  • Device and other identifiers (e.g. Advertising ID, installation ID) — used for push notifications, analytics, and app functionality.
  • Account data (email/name, if provided) — used for authentication and personalization.
  • App activity — used for analytics and product improvement.
  • Purchases — used for payment processing and restoring access.

If you don’t have an account

In guest mode, no account is created. However, you may still send a request to delete analytics data linked to your device. Please include the approximate installation date and device model in your support email so we can identify the records.
